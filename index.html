About Me

Hi, I’m Supriya Bhange—a beach lover, dreamer, and the creative mind behind my Instagram feed. I share my own photos and edits, capturing life’s sunniest moments and celebrating self-love with every post. My page is a reflection of my journey—full of ocean waves, colorful edits, and the confidence to love myself just as I am. Travel is my ultimate dream, and one day, I want to explore every corner of this beautiful world. For now, I’m collecting memories from every beach I visit and inspiring others to find joy in their own adventures. Follow along as I turn everyday moments into art, chase sunsets, and work towards my biggest dream: traveling the globe and sharing my story with you!

My Style

  • “All eyes on my bitches—I am the prettiest in the room. But don’t get it twisted: I love attention, not objectification.”

  • I love skin-fit and short clothes that show off my curves 🔥✨
  • Bright colors that complement my body are my favorite 🎨🌈
